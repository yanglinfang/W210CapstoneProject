Experience Patent Similarity Match Engine With IP-mine

IP-mine patent similarity match tool is web-based aid available for patent lawyer offices, patent department within organizations, possibly USPTO to decipher new claims made by inventors. We will provide early input for inventors, patent lawyers about the similarity of their claims with respect to history of claims that have been approved.

Our tool is very simple, fast and accurate to use. This helps everyone involved in the patenting of a new claim-idea complete the process in a speedy manner. Faster process means early monetization of new claim.
